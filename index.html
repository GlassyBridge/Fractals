Exploring Fractals

Fractals are intricate and fascinating structures that exhibit self-similarity at various scales. They are created by repeating simple mathematical processes. Fractals are not just beautiful; they also appear in nature, art, and various scientific fields such as computer graphics, physics, and biology. Their properties can model real-world phenomena, from the branching of trees to the fractal patterns on leaves and coastlines.
